Rules of Pig Game

Pig is a simple dice game. Here are the rules:

  1. Each turn, a player rolls a die as many times as they wish, adding the dice value to their current total.
  2. If the player rolls a 1, their current total for the turn is lost, and it becomes the next player's turn.
  3. The player can choose to "Hold," which adds their current total to their global score and passes the turn to the next player.
  4. The first player to reach 100 points on their global score wins the game.
  5. Have fun and GOOD LUCK!
